Tips / Pet Care

Regular Brushing Matters

Brushing your dog regularly helps prevent matting, removes loose fur, and keeps their coat healthy and shiny. For long-haired breeds, brushing daily is ideal, while short-haired dogs can be brushed 1–2 times per week.

How Often Should You Bathe?

Over-bathing can strip natural oils from your pet’s coat. Most dogs benefit from a bath every 4–6 weeks, depending on their lifestyle and coat type. Always use dog-specific shampoo to avoid skin irritation.

Nail Trimming & Paw Care

Long nails can cause discomfort and affect your dog’s posture. Regular nail trims help prevent pain and injury. If you’re unsure, our groomers are happy to take care of it safely for you.
