Tips & Pet Care

Simple grooming and care tips to keep your dog healthy and happy

Regular Brushing Matters

Brushing your dog regularly helps prevent matting, removes loose fur, and keeps their coat healthy and shiny. Long-haired breeds benefit from daily brushing, while short-haired dogs can be brushed 1–2 times per week.

How Often Should You Bathe?

Over-bathing can strip natural oils from your dog’s coat. Most dogs need a bath every 4–6 weeks depending on their lifestyle. Always use dog-specific shampoo to avoid skin irritation.

Nail Trimming & Paw Care

Long nails can cause discomfort and affect your dog’s posture. Regular nail trimming helps prevent pain and injury. If you’re unsure, our professional groomers can safely help.
